About Me: Jaiyah Davis

Hi, I’m Jaiyah, a detail-obsessed QA professional specializing in Health Payer data integrity. My journey into tech is driven by a focus on precision, a first-generation hunger for learning, and a deep appreciation for workflow efficiency.

The Story Behind the Tester

My unique perspective is shaped by hands-on experience in Health Benefits Enrollment and a personal passion for Women’s Care and Oncology—domains where data integrity is paramount to patient health. From managing complex employer plans and rates, I learned that the smallest data entry error can cascade into significant issues. This background naturally led me to Quality Assurance: a field where INTJ analysis and structured testing are used to proactively secure high-stakes workflows.

Beyond the Keyboard

As a devoted mother and an introvert, I bring powerful prioritization and organizational skills to every project, treating every test plan with the meticulous care of managing a busy household. I am committed to continuous learning in automation (Cypress) and regularly dedicate time to volunteering, driven by the belief that robust systems improve real-world well-being.
